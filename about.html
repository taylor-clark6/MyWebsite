About Me!

Nice to meet you! My name is Taylor Clark, and I am a sophomore in the Lincoln Center Honors Program at Fordham University. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in French and Francophone Studies; I have a passion for languages of all types! I am interested in programming and software development and would like to pursue a career in Software Engineering.

I have experience coding in C++, Java, JavaScript, Swift, Python, and HTML, and I have a JavaScript Level 1 Certification from CodeHS. I was also a Virtual Experience Participant in the Goldman Sachs FinTech Engineering Virtual Experience Program as well as the JPMorgan Chase & Co. Software Engineering Virtual Experience, both presented by Forage. I also am proficient in using Git and GitHub, Figma, and the Agile approach to software development.

I am a member of the Fordham University Computer Science Society, Google Student Developers Club, and Girls Who Code College Loop. In Girls Who Code, we are currently developing a mindful food-journaling app, which we hope to complete this semester. It has been exhilarating learning what goes into developing a functional app, as well as working with a passionate, collaborative, and supportive group of women all dedicated to a profession in technology.

In addition, I am a member of the National Center for Women and Information Technology (NCWIT), and I was awarded the Aspirations in Computing Connecticut Affiliate Award in 2021.

I am eager to expand my knowledge and experience in technology and software engineering! Please do not hesitate to contact me at any time with inquiries or requests!
